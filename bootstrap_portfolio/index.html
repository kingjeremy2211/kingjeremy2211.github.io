About Me

I am a proud father of 3 children, two boys and a girl, ages 10,8, and 5. I switched to the weekend shift after we had our first child, to avoid paying half my salary on day care. I work Friday thru Sunday, 12 hour shifts, 5am to 5pm.

My current position is a Machine Operator IIII, which is the highest level possible at my current company. This means I have been certified to run over 25 machines in the card manufacturing/printing industry. 14 years of learning and training new operators has given me lots to be proud of.


Ever since I can remember, I've been interested in how things work. My mom used to find me in my room late at night, dismantling the vcr, tv, or stereo, just to put it back together again. I quickly earned the nickname "Mr.Fixit". If anyone in my household, or immediate family, needed something fixed, I was the first one willing and eager to give it my best.

Nothing has changed since then. I have learned many skills throghout the years, including:
